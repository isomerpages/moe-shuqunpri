<h4><strong>Interested applicants must meet the following criteria:</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Singapore citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident (SPR) for stallholder
and stall</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Committed to providing good service and quality food with prices determined
in consultation with the</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to provide cooked food that meets the health / nutritional standards
set by the Health Promotion Board (HPB) under the <a href="https://www.hpb.gov.sg/schools/school-programmes/healthy-meals-in-schools-programme" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Healthy Meals in Schools Programme</a>.
This includes providing a serving of fruits with the meal.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to maintain high standard of food safety and personal</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Healthy and fit to operate the</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Preferably possess some experience operating a food stall in a school
environment</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>Upon successful award, the stallholder is expected to:</strong></h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Possess the WSQ Basic Food Hygiene Certificate (SFA). Any stall assistant(s)
will be required to possess the WSQ Basic Food Hygiene Certificate (SFA)
too.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Apply for a food stall license from Singapore Food Agency (SFA).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Commence operation of the stall as soon as</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Operate the stall based on the school-approved menu, from Monday to Friday
during school term Should the need arise, the stallholder is expected to
operate on selected days during school holidays.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Shortlisted applicants will be notified for an interview and will be required
to provide food sampling for the School Canteen Committee at the applicant’s
own expense.</p>
